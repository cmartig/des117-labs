Lucky

Lucky is a sweet puppy that loves to play and be cuddled. Lucky was found in a field with herthree other siblings, who are all in forever homes now. Unfortunately Lucky has worms and her shelter is unable to pay for the necessary medication. Lucky also needs a new bed and leash. These items will all go with her when she finds her forever home and will aid in the adoption process! Click "Donate" to help Lucky out! DONATE

Beau

Beau is one of our favorite boys. Beau was found underneath a car all by himself as a 6-week old puppy. Because of his harsh start in this world, Beau is a little skittish towards new people and needs socialization training. The amazing shelter he is at has done all they can internally and want to source an outsider to come and help with training. Unfortunately the shelter cannot afford to do this, and it is a necessary step in order for Beau to find his forever home! Click "Donate" to help Beau out!
