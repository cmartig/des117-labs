Welcome!

Welcome to Paw-Fect Pals, a crowd sourcing website for dogs in need world-wide. We strive to provide funds for unforeseen medical needs and living expenses of shelter dogs. If you are a dog lover but are unable to make the large commitment of adopting a dog, then this is the place for you! Click "Go Fetch" and check out some pals who need funding today!
